The story behind the shop

About WanderingYarns

A little shop born from two loves: the meditative joy of crochet, and the thrill of finding something special while travelling somewhere new.

Where it all started

WanderingYarns started as a hobby — making little crocheted creatures for friends and family. Then came the travels, and with them, the souvenirs: small objects picked up from markets, shops, and roadside stalls that felt too good to leave behind.

Eventually both worlds collided into one small shop. Every item here is either made by hand or personally collected — nothing is mass-produced or ordered from a catalogue.

Each piece has a story. That's the whole point.
